How It Works

From onboarding to audit-ready in 30 days

A simple, structured process that gets your compliance program running — and keeps it that way continuously.

1
Compliance Assessment
We audit your current policies, workflows, and systems to identify gaps across every applicable framework.
2
Platform Configuration
We configure REPrieve to your organization — loading your policies, mapping workflows, and setting up AI agent rules.
3
Team Onboarding
Role-based training for clinical, compliance, and operations staff. Average onboarding time: 3 days.
4
Ongoing AI Monitoring
Your AI agents run continuously — monitoring regulatory changes, flagging gaps, and keeping you perpetually audit-ready.
